About Shri Siddhivinayak Temple

Located in the serene village of Chandguli, near Yellapur in Karnataka, the Shri Siddhi Vinayaka Temple is a sacred place of worship dedicated to Lord Ganesha.


Lord Siddhivinayak is known as the "Granter of Wishes" and the "Remover of Obstacles." Devotees from the local community and surrounding regions visit this temple to seek blessings for success in their personal and professional lives. The temple serves as a spiritual anchor for the community, hosting daily pujas and special celebrations during Ganesh Chaturthi.


The peaceful atmosphere of Chandguli, combined with the divine presence of the deity, offers a unique opportunity for meditation and inner peace. We invite you to visit and experience the grace of Lord Ganesha.
